Children

Kids Ministry is designed for the purpose of helping children know, love, and follow Jesus. Our Kids Ministry is deeply committed to seeing kids reached with the gospel. We believe children are very capable of understanding the truths of the gospel and have the ability to repent of their sin and trust Christ for their salvation.

Youth

Men

Men, are you challenged by the balancing act of life? It doesn’t have to be that way. Join with other men to talk about the real issues of life and what the Bible has to say about them. Each week is a different study that is video driven with discussion and is open to all men in our community.
