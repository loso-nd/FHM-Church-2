Online Tithe and Offering

Simple and secure. Give a single gift or schedule recurring giving using your bank account, debit or credit card. Once you create your account, you can give in just seconds.
Keep in mind that giving by electronic check carries the lowest fees for the church.

Automate your tithing

Automating your giving by setting up a recurring gift is a great way to consistently put God first in your finances. Recurring giving is easy and will take you less than two minutes to set up. Simply choose your schedule and amount to start automating your giving today.
