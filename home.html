Welcome To First Haitian Missionary Church
It's Nice To Meet You

WELCOME!

If you are a first-time visitor, please be sure to notify one of the ushers when you arrive. If you bring your children to Kids Church, let one of our ushers know and you will be directed to their classes. Be sure to fill out a Welcome Card. If you need assistance, feel free to ask one of our ushers or greeters and they will be happy to help you!

WE ARE GLAD YOU'RE HERE!

Who we are

First Haitian Missionary is a place of life and hope. We are everyday people with families, jobs, and responsibilities. However, we are extremely passionate about developing a relationship with Christ.

What to expect

At First Haitian Missionary, you will find a community of people who love God and care about one another. When you arrive, expect to be welcomed and seated by our ushers. Our services consist of worship and preaching of the Bible. If you have children, they are welcome to participate in our children’s service.

When we meet

Prayer service is held on Tuesdays at 7:30PM. Friday night Veil de nuit begins at 7:30PM. On Sundays, starting at 9:00 AM we have Sunday School then Sunday Service follow immediately at 10:30 AM. We also have night service on sundays at 7:30PM and Youth Bible study on thursdays at 7:30PM. We believe we are most powerful when we gather together in the presense of God.

Why visit

We believe church should be a place where you meet and learn about Christ. Where God is, lives are restored, relationships are mended, and hope is found. At FHM we hope you will connect with God, make good friends, and find fulfillment in areas of your life. Come to fellowship with us.
